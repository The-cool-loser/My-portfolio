Kindergarten Sister

Original from Heathers by Kevin Murphy and Laurence O'Keefe

Inspired by Deltarune

When I was young I had an older sister
She was nice, she liked a thrilling tale
She calmed me down and told me not to worry
For she promised to protect me well
That night I slept as if I were a fawn still
Knowing she will always have my back

If I fell down

Then the day I distinctly remember
My dad, Rudolph told me to sit down
I was naive, could not tell what had happened
But right then I asked him “when is Dess”
He told me something I can’t bear repeating
But suffice to say that in a way

My sis fell down

Then at next year, a spelling bee
They asked me to spell her name
I could not bear to think so I simply just lost
The winner than went bezerk, started bragging for years to come
That he was way smarter than I
Even though he’s not

Now again my Dad is in a checkup
And for now it’s simply me and mom
We don’t really need to worry this time
But we just can’t bear to be alone

But in this world, where everything is different
They show me how powerful I am
So I’m face to face with weird creatures
And this time it’s them who’re falling done

Every death and every freeze
Is making me more strong
Now I know how it feels
At the side of success
So I cast a few spells
On many a monsters
And everything’s perfect at last
